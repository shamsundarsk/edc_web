## üö® Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Keyword Stuffing**: Don't overuse keywords unnaturally
2. **Duplicate Content**: Ensure all content is unique
3. **Slow Loading**: Keep page speed under 3 seconds
4. **Missing Alt Text**: Always add descriptive alt text to images
5. **Broken Links**: Regularly check and fix broken links
6. **No Mobile Optimization**: Ensure mobile-first design
7. **Ignoring Analytics**: Monitor and act on data insights
